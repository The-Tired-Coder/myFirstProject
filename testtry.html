Breakfast Casarole

ingredients

  • 2 pounds hashbrowns
  • 1 pound pork sausage
  • 5 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 tsp of onion powder
  • 1/8 tsp garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 ounces shreded chedder cheese

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350 degrees f. Grease a 2 quart baking dish. place the hashbrowns in the bottom of the baking dish.

Step 2

Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the sausage until done. Meanwhile whisk together the eggs, milk, onion powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper, and pour over the potatoes. Layer with half the chedder cheese, the sausage, and the remaining chedder cheese. Cover with aluminum foil.

Step 3

Bake in the preheated oven for one hour. Remove cover; return casarole to the oven and bake until a knife inserted into the center comes out clean,about 10 minutes. let stand for five minutes before devouring.
